✅ tomp3 is installed!

CLI — open a new terminal and run:

tomp3 --help

Menu bar app — look for the waveform icon (⌃) in your menu bar.

Finder extension — to enable right-click conversion:

  1. Open System Settings → Privacy & Security → Extensions
  2. Click Finder Extensions
  3. Enable tomp3

Source & updates: github.com/aramb-dev/tomp3
